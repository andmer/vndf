About

Von Neumann Defense Force is a multiplayer game in early development. It's not even remotely playable right now.

Download

The client is only available for 64-bit Linux. It works on Ubuntu 12.04 and quite possibly other distributions and versions.

Download the client.

After the download, make the client executable and start it:

$ chmod a+x vndf-client
$ ./vndf-client reineke.vndf.de

Controls

In the game, you can control the camera with the cursor keys.
